03 · How to use

Walk up. Touch, or talk.

No manual. No instructions screen. The wall is designed to be picked up in seconds by any first-time guest — and deeper on a second visit.

Step 01
Approach

Walk up.
The wall greets you.

Guest name is auto-picked from the URL (?client=…). Tamil and English greeting fire in the first second.

"Welcome, Infosys."
Step 02
Tap

Tap a story bubble.
Open three facts.

Any of the sixteen Chennai topics. A card slides in with three rich paragraphs — history, culture, living tradition.

Tap the Fort, tap the Marina, tap Kolam art — each opens a different story.
Step 03
Speak

Say "Hey Nova."
Ask anything.

The mic wakes on the wake-word. Ask about projects, Wongdoody, or Chennai itself. Conversational replies, cited sources.

"Hey Nova, show me retail AI work." · "Hey Nova, when did we join Infosys?"
Step 04
Explore

Tap Explore.
See our history + work.

"EXPLORE WONGDOODY" enters the Legacy screen. Open the side menu → Continue to reach the Work dashboard with sixteen case studies.

Any hover tells you what to tap next — the wall never leaves you stuck.
